Collecting the River

Water is a resource worth saving. Much like land, food, and time, water's value is intrinsic and essential. The RiverFoundation believes that every person on the planet should have the ability to access this vital resource. Owning and operating water collection facilities provides the RiverFoundation with the opportunity to secure significant amounts of this valuable resource in order to decentralize its distribution throughout the globe

Sharing the river

The RiverFoundation will always hold enough water in their reserves to fully endorse every RiverCoin in circulation. Furthermore, the RiverFoundation will produce and distribute a consistent supply of water [~50k-liters/day?] to numerous locations (RiverStations?) across the planet where it will be redeemable in exchange for RiverCoin. The RiverCoin Network finally makes it possible for every person on Earth to have [stable] access to the world’s most precious resource—Water.
